# Product definition

## Problem

Users need to scan uncertain events, understand market consensus, inspect resolution rules and express an outcome preference without being overwhelmed by exchange-terminal complexity.

## Product principles

1. Event comprehension before trading mechanics.
2. Probability and liquidity before decoration.
3. Resolution rules and deadlines remain discoverable.
4. Money-like actions require deliberate confirmation and trust cues.
5. Mobile discovery is a first-class path.

## Core journeys

- Browse and filter markets.
- Compare outcome probabilities.
- Open a market and inspect trend, rules and liquidity.
- Select an outcome and preview an order.
- Review positions and activity after authentication is integrated.

## Non-goals in this starter

- Real-money execution, custody, wallet signing or KYC.
- Resolution oracle implementation.
- Regulatory assertions.
- Pixel-copying a third-party brand.
